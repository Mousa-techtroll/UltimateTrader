Entry Engines

Three specialized engines replace the flat plugin list. Each engine contains multiple modes organized in a priority cascade — at most one signal per engine per bar. The Day Router activates engines based on the classified day type.

💧
Liquidity Engine
Smart money order flow — sweep, displace, mitigate. 4 modes, priority cascade.
▲ Displacement P1 Highest
Liquidity sweep beyond swing high/low followed by a displacement candle (body > 1.8x ATR). Confirms structural shift (CHoCH/BOS).
Quality: A+/A SL: Sweep wick TP: Adaptive 2-3R
■ OB Retest P2
Order block retest with rejection candle. Price returns to validated OB zone and shows rejection wick (> 60% of range).
Quality: A/B+ SL: OB boundary TP: Next OB/swing
▬ FVG Mitigation P3
Fair value gap fill with rejection. Price enters FVG zone (> 50pt gap), shows rejection, trend alignment confirmed. 8-bar cooldown between signals.
Quality: A/B+ SL: FVG opposite PF 3.14 (18 trades) Cooldown: 8 bars
✨ SFP P4 Lowest
Swing Failure Pattern — price sweeps a swing level then closes back inside. Optional RSI divergence boost for higher conviction.
Quality: B+/B SL: Sweep extreme Divergence: Optional

Session Engine
Time-gated session plays — 5 modes, mutually exclusive by GMT hour.
🌈 Asian Range Build 00-07 GMT

Quality: A+ SL: FVG boundary ICT Concept

8-Step Risk Pipeline

Every signal passes through an 8-step multiplicative risk pipeline. Each step independently adjusts position size. The final lot size is the product of all multipliers applied to the base quality-tier risk allocation.

1

Quality Tier Allocation

Base risk % determined by setup grade: A+ (1.0%), A (0.8%), B+ (0.6%), B (0.5%).

0.5% – 1.0%
2

Consecutive Loss Scaling

Reduces risk after losing streaks: 2-3 losses → 0.75x, 4+ losses → 0.50x.

0.50x – 1.0x
3

Volatility Regime Adjustment

5-tier volatility regime: Very Low (1.0x), Low (0.92x), Normal (1.0x), High (0.85x), Extreme (0.65x).

0.65x – 1.0x
4

Short Protection

Gold bullish bias: short trades get 0.5x risk multiplier. Additional ADX and macro filters for bear setups.

0.5x (shorts only)
5

Health-Based Risk

System health monitor adjusts risk: Excellent (1.0x), Good (0.9x), Fair (0.7x), Degraded (0.5x), Critical (0x).

0.0x – 1.0x
6

Session Quality Gate

Auto-reduce risk in sessions with poor execution quality. Blocks entries below 0.25, halves risk below 0.50.

0.0x – 1.0x
7

Shock Volatility Override

Detects shock bars (range > 2.0x ATR) and blocks new entries. Protects against flash crashes and news spikes.

0.0x or 1.0x
8

Position & Margin Limits

Max positions (5), max total exposure (5%), max margin usage (80%), max lot multiplier (10x), daily trade cap (5).

Trailing Stop System

Three broker SL modification modes control how the trailing system communicates with the broker. The actual trail calculation uses one of 7 strategies (ATR, Swing, SAR, Chandelier, Stepped, Hybrid, Smart).

■ Batched Mode

Default & recommended. SL modifications only sent at key R-multiple levels. Minimizes broker calls, avoids requotes, preserves edge by not revealing trailing logic.

Recommended

▲ Aggressive Mode

Every trailing update immediately modifies the broker SL. Maximum protection but high broker call frequency. May trigger requotes on volatile sessions.

High Frequency

☐ Revert (Disabled)

Pre-fix behavior — no broker SL modification at all. Trail calculated internally but never sent. For debugging or brokers that penalize SL changes.
